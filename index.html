When you want to learn in IT theres alot of different sectors and different applications. The different sectors in IT are Software developer, Data Analysts, Cyber security and lots more. Please go and read our information about our different sectors below.

Software Developer

In the contemporary landscape, the duties and responsibilities of a software developer include designing, creating, testing, and monitoring software programs and applications. These professionals now perform their work across many industries

Software Developer

Data Analyst

A data analyst works with the pertinent stakeholders to identify appropriate and necessary data and reporting requirements, and then they're tasked with turning raw data into relevant and meaningful insights

Data Analyst

Cybersecurity

Cyber security is how individuals and organisations reduce the risk of cyber attack. Cyber security's core function is to protect the devices we all use (smartphones, laptops, tablets and computers), and the services we access - both online and at work - from theft or damage.

Cybersecurity

IT support & Administration

Providing technical assistance to employees. Updating and maintaining company systems and networks. Installing and configuring software. Managing user access permissions and credentials.

IT support & Administration

Cloud Computing & DevOps

When DevOps teams work in the cloud, they enjoy easier access to scalable hardware resources that can help build, test, and deploy new updates and offerings more quickly.

Cloud Computing & DevOps

Networking & Infrastructure

Network infrastructure refers to the hardware and software that enable network connectivity and communication between users, devices, apps, the internet, and more.

Networking & Infrastructure

IT Projects & Business Roles

IT project managers play a crucial role by delegating tasks to hit milestones and keeping communication open among all team members. Use team collaboration software to ensure everyone is on the same page about who's doing what by when
